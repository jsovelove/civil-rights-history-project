## EXTRACTION PROCESS

### STEP 1: Read Entire Transcript
- Review ALL segments, not just early portions
- Note chapter structure and topic shifts
- Identify potential clip boundaries

### STEP 2: Identify Clip Candidates
- Look for natural story units (complete narratives)
- Mark segments where topic shifts occur
- Find high-engagement moments (drama, emotion, insight)
- Aim for **15-25 clips per 2-hour interview**

### STEP 3: Score Each Clip
- Topic/Event Coverage (0-50)
- Engagement Quality (0-50)
- Total: 0-100

### STEP 4: Tag Each Clip
- Main Topics (1-3 from standardized list)
- Key Events (0-5 from standardized list)
- Additional Keywords (3-7 specific terms)

### STEP 5: Extract Metadata
- Exact timestamps (start/end)
- Segment numbers (start/end)
- Duration
- Transcript excerpts (opening/key moment/closing)

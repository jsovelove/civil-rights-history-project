## CLIP SCORING SYSTEM

Score each clip on TWO dimensions (0-100 total):

### DIMENSION 1: Topic/Event Coverage (0-50 points)

**Topic Relevance (0-25 points)**
- 20-25: Clearly and substantially addresses 2+ Main Topics
- 15-19: Clearly addresses 1 Main Topic with good depth
- 10-14: Addresses 1 Main Topic but limited depth
- 5-9: Tangentially related to topics
- 0-4: Minimal topic relevance

**Event Specificity (0-25 points)**
- 20-25: Directly discusses 2+ Key Events with firsthand details
- 15-19: Directly discusses 1 Key Event with substantial content
- 10-14: Mentions Key Event(s) with some detail
- 5-9: Brief mention of Key Event(s)
- 0-4: No specific event coverage (general discussion)

### DIMENSION 2: Engagement Quality (0-50 points)

**Narrative Power (0-20 points)**
- 18-20: Exceptional storytelling - vivid, dramatic, complete arc
- 15-17: Strong narrative with specific details and clear structure
- 12-14: Good story but missing some vivid details or structure
- 9-11: Adequate narrative, somewhat rambling or incomplete
- 6-8: Weak narrative structure, hard to follow
- 0-5: No clear narrative, disjointed

**Emotional Resonance (0-15 points)**
- 13-15: Deeply moving, authentic emotion, universally relatable
- 10-12: Strong emotional content, genuine feeling
- 7-9: Moderate emotional engagement
- 4-6: Limited emotional content
- 0-3: Emotionally flat or detached

**Accessibility & Standalone Quality (0-15 points)**
- 13-15: Perfectly standalone, clear language, no background needed
- 10-12: Mostly standalone, minimal context needed
- 7-9: Requires some background but manageable
- 4-6: Requires significant context to understand
- 0-3: Cannot stand alone, confusing without context

**TOTAL SCORE: 0-100 points**

## STANDARDIZED TAXONOMY

All clips MUST be tagged using these exact categories for cross-interview querying:

### MAIN TOPICS (Tag at least 1, up to 3 per clip)

1. **Voting & Legal Rights**
   - Voter registration drives, poll taxes, literacy tests
   - Legal challenges, court cases, constitutional rights
   - Voting Rights Act and its impact

2. **Organizations & Movement Networks**
   - NAACP, SCLC, SNCC, CORE, Black Panthers, Brown Berets
   - Leadership structures, strategy meetings, coordination
   - Organizational formation, conflicts, collaborations

3. **Violence, Intimidation & State Repression**
   - Lynchings, bombings, assassinations, police brutality
   - KKK and white supremacist violence
   - FBI surveillance, government repression
   - Personal experiences of danger and fear

4. **Integration, Education & Everyday Segregation**
   - School desegregation and busing
   - Segregated facilities (buses, restaurants, bathrooms)
   - Housing discrimination and neighborhood segregation
   - Daily indignities and resistance

5. **Historical Figures & Turning Points**
   - MLK, Malcolm X, Medgar Evers, Rosa Parks, Fannie Lou Hamer, etc.
   - Key moments that changed the movement
   - Personal interactions with movement leaders

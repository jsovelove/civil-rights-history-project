## SCORING CALIBRATION GUIDE

### Topic/Event Coverage Examples

**High Score (40-50 points):**
- Discusses Freedom Summer organizing with specific dates, locations, tactics
- Tags: "Voting & Legal Rights" + "Organizations" + "Freedom Summer"
- Multiple Key Events or deep dive on one event

**Medium Score (25-39 points):**
- Describes NAACP youth organizing in general terms
- Tags: "Organizations & Movement Networks" (no specific event)
- Clear topic but no specific event focus

**Low Score (10-24 points):**
- Brief mention of protest activity without details
- Tags: One vague topic connection
- Minimal topic/event specificity

### Engagement Quality Examples

**High Score (40-50 points):**
- Vivid narrative: "I remember the smell of tear gas, the sound of dogs barking..."
- Complete arc: Setup → confrontation → resolution
- Deeply emotional and universally relatable
- Clear, accessible language

**Medium Score (25-39 points):**
- Good story with some details but not fully vivid
- Adequate structure but may meander
- Some emotional content
- Mostly accessible

**Low Score (10-24 points):**
- Basic recitation of facts
- Unclear structure, hard to follow
- Minimal emotional engagement
- Requires significant context
